About Us

Established in 2017, Bangalore Charitable Trust (BCT) is a non-profit dedicated to palliative and geriatric care. We offer hospice services, old age homes, home visits, and pain management education, led by skilled medical professionals with a mission to serve with compassion.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To alleviate the physical and psychological discomfort of patients with incurable cancer and terminal illness through compassionate care.

Our Mission

To support patients and families with dignity, ensuring they live the remainder of their lives with comfort, care, and emotional well-being.

Our Objectives & Core Values

  • Deliver compassionate palliative care to the terminally ill.
  • Provide holistic support—medical, emotional, spiritual.
  • Uphold values of Compassion, Care, Commitment, Competence.
  • Train healthcare professionals in community-based care.
  • Collaborate with government bodies and NGOs to scale outreach.

Care Impact

We’ve cared for over 190 patients—mostly seniors suffering from cancer, Alzheimer’s, paralysis, and chronic illness. With limited capacity, we aim to expand our services and continue offering high-quality, dignified care to those in need.
