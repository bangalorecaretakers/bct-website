About Us

Founded in 2017, Bangalore Charitable Trust (BCT) is a non-profit organization providing end-of-life care with a heart. Our services include palliative care, home visits, old age support, and hospice education. Backed by medical professionals, our mission is driven by empathy and dignity.

Our Vision

To alleviate physical and emotional pain for terminally ill cancer patients and provide dignified care for those in their final stages of life.

Our Mission

To affirm life, provide physical and emotional care to patients, and support families through the challenges of life-threatening illness.

Our Objectives & Values

  • Deliver quality palliative care for the terminally ill
  • Offer emotional, spiritual, and social support
  • Promote Compassion, Care, Commitment, Competence
  • Train healthcare workers in community palliative management
  • Collaborate with government bodies & NGOs for extended care
